-Drag and drop the vfx effect onto the screne/hierarchy.
-The height can be adjusted by changing the y Transform position of your particle System.
-Size can also be adjusted from the particle properties inside inspector.
-Prefabs are ready to go for any scene, just drag and drop them into the scene.
- Loop can be turned off by turning the loop property to off in inspector view, this should be done to all child objects.
-You can also increase or decrease the amount of emittion of any effect being spawned at a time,
 by going to Inspector Emission, and change the value of Rate over time.
